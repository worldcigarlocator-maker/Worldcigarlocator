About World Cigar Locator

The idea for World Cigar Locator was born from a simple need: the desire to always know where to find a good cigar, no matter where life takes you. Traveling to a new city or exploring another country should never mean missing out on the comfort of a cigar lounge or the charm of a well-stocked store.

While the vision spans the whole world, the feeling we aim for is personal, even intimate. It’s not about numbers or scale — it’s about those small, memorable moments that cigars often create. In the end, this isn’t just a global map. It’s a way of shrinking the wide world down into your own little bubble, where the next good smoke is always within reach.

World Cigar Locator is still young and growing step by step. With help from members, contributors, and cigar lovers everywhere, we’re building a resource that reflects the culture, community, and passion for cigars across continents — yet always connects back to you.
